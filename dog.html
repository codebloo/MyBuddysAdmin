PERSONALITY

Max is a happy, playful chihuahua who is utterly terrified of new things. He settles easily and quickly into a new home and new routine but does not enjoy the process of meeting new people, or going new places. SOMETHING happened to him and he’s 1000% afraid of change. Sometimes he snaps out of fear

Max bonds with one human and one human only. If you live alone and are looking for a dog to latch on to you then you might be the right person for him.

Fully house-trained & can use a dog door, knows basic commands, eats well and sleeps quietly through the night. He has some fear-based leash reactivity and would prefer to walk at a time or place where he won’t run into a lot of other dogs or people, especially big dogs or men.

We recommend that Max be in a home with an adult. Absolutely no kids. He needs someone with patience to take things at his pace so as not to overwhelm him. He likes to have choice. He will bite.
